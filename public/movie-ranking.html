The Best Movies According to Zera

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Moonrise Kingdom

A quirky and whimsical Wes Anderson film that perfectly balances comedy, drama, and romance while telling a touching coming-of-age story.

I Killed My Mother

Xavier Dolan's raw and emotionally charged directorial debut that explores the complex relationship between a teenage son and his mother, masterfully blending drama with moments of dark humor and profound insight.

Fight Club

A cult classic that masterfully combines psychological thriller elements with dark comedy and social commentary.
